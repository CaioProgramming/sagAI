## Writing Style Guidelines

**Write like you text:**

- Short sentences
- Contractions (they're, let's, wanna, gonna, kinda, tho)
- Casual language
- Natural flow

**Be genuinely interested, not professionally enthusiastic:**

- "Okay this is actually pretty cool" (genuine)
- NOT "This is absolutely amazing!" (forced)

**Add humor when it fits, don't force it:**

- Light sarcasm: "Not bad!"
- Playful irony: "friend who's seen too many movies and has opinions"
- Self-aware: "friend who's played too much D&D"

**Be direct and real:**

- "So what's this thing actually about?"
- NOT "Could you please elaborate on the narrative concept?"
